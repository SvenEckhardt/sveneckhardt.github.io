About

Modern Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence exceed human capabilities on countless tasks. However, in many practical settings, these capabilities can be used, only if humans rely on the AI output. Therefore, my research focuses on the human-centered use of AI-based systems and the topic of reliance on AI systems.
